How to play?


The aim of the game is to help the Pac-Man to eat all the pac-dots and earn the highest score. On the other hand, beware the ghosts that are looking for you. Pick up some pac-powerups,

if you find yourself in a trouble and eat them to be able to eat the ghosts.

For controlling the game use the "W", "A", "S", "D" keys, however, you can change them in the settings.

Good luck!
